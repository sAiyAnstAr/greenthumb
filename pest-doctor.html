About this Pest

Aphids are small, sap-sucking insects that can rapidly multiply and cause stunted growth, leaf curling, and the spread of plant viruses. They secrete 'honeydew', a sticky substance that encourages the growth of black sooty mold.

Organic Repellents

Neem Oil Spray

Mix 2 tsp of cold-pressed neem oil with 1 tsp of mild soap in a quart of water. Spray directly on the undersides of leaves.

Introduce Ladybugs

Ladybugs are natural predators that can consume up to 50 aphids a day. Release them in the cool of the evening.

Strong Water Stream

A firm blast from the garden hose can physically dislodge aphids from sturdy plants without chemicals.
