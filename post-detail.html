Fiddle Leaf Fig leaves turning brown?

I've noticed these spots appearing on the lower leaves over the past week. I water once a week and it gets indirect bright light. Is this root rot or something else entirely? Any advice would be so appreciated — it's my favourite plant!

Common cause: overwatering or root rot

Brown spots on lower leaves with yellowing edges usually indicate overwatering. Check if soil is staying wet between waterings and ensure the pot has drainage holes.
