Why are the lower leaves of my Echeveria turning translucent and mushy?

I haven't watered it in two weeks, but the base looks damp. Is this root rot or something else?

Master Gardener Dr. Silas Green

This sounds like classic overwatering leading to edema or early-stage root rot. Even if you haven't watered recently, the soil medium might be retaining too much moisture. Check the drainage holes and consider repotting in a 50/50 mix of cactus soil and perlite immediately.
